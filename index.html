How it works

Three taps between you and an honest number.

01

Start your shift

Tap once when you clock on. Tollby measures your miles by GPS — only while you're on the clock, and I can't ever see it.

02

Drive

Work however you work, across Uber, Lyft, DoorDash, and more. Your miles add up automatically.

03

See your take

Stop the shift, drop in your earnings, and get your real hourly net and your running mileage deduction.

●

Private by design

Tollby runs entirely on your device and your own iCloud. No account, no login, no servers. Your location is used only to measure your shift miles — it never leaves your phone, and we never see your data.
